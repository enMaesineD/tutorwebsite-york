Who we are

The Branksome Hall tutoring club is devoted to providing help for all those in need academically! We have a variety of subjects that our experienced tutors can teach. Most popular subjects that people need help with include math, science, and french. Last year, our tutors tutored almost 200 hours! Contact vhuang@branksome.on.ca if any assistance is needed or if you just want to talk about your future tutoring plans!

How do you start tutoring?

On our website, there is a "Register" button on the bar at the top for all tutors. If you need any further assistance, please contact Vita Huang, a grade 10 student at Branksome Hall, at vhuang@branksome.on.ca!

How do I request a tutor?

On our website, there is a button at the top labelled "Request". Please fill out the form attached stating the times you are available and the subjects you need help in so that the admins can pair you up with your tutor. Expect see an email in your inbox with the tutor information some days later!
